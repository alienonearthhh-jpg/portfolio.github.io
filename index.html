About Me

Works Contacts

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and interaction design master's student at UTS, with a background that spans marketing, visual communication, and event production. I'm drawn to the space where design meets people — building experiences that are grounded, considered, and human.

My practice is informed by film photography and a careful eye for visual storytelling. I bring creative rigour and collaborative energy to every project — and I'm always learning.
