About GSS

Crafting premium
active sportswear.

Global Sportive Solution Co., Ltd. is dedicated to serving professional, amateur, and club athletes worldwide. Our founder brings over 30 years of textile manufacturing expertise — collaborating with Adidas, Nike, Puma, Pearl Izumi, Rapha, Specialized, and many more.

#Our Mission

Provide high value-added quality services to our customers and bring innovation to the sports world.

#Our Vision

To build a comfortable workplace where people can come to find and discover with us the best product they need.

#Our Approach

From sourcing innovative, sustainable fabrics to crafting gear that combines functionality with style — we pursue excellence at every step.

Advancing
the sport.

We are actively experimenting to advance the cycling and activewear industry — improving production processes and exploring new options to enhance athletes' performance and longevity.

In collaboration with university students in Germany, we exchange expertise with innovative discoveries to uncover new levels of comfort in intensive training. Lab and field testing with professional athletes shapes every refinement.
