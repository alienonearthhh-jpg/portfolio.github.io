Overview

An ongoing creative relationship with Doorplant, a Bangkok-based guitar-pop band known for their warm, analogue aesthetic. The work spans album cover photography and illustration, EP artwork, film band photography, and music video production.

The visual language across the releases draws on found imagery, film grain, and a deliberately unhurried sense of time — mirroring the band's sound.
